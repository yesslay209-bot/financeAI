What Interest Is

Interest is the cost of borrowing money or the reward for saving it. Think of it as "rent" for using someone else's cash.

Why It Matters

Higher interest means loans cost more and savings grow faster. Even a 1% difference can change your balance by thousands over time.

Helpful Habit:
Avoid high-interest debt like credit cards (often 20%+) while letting your savings grow in accounts that pay you back.
