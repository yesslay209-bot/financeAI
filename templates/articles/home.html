Before You Start

Stable income and a solid emergency fund help reduce the stress of homeownership. Lenders also look closely at your credit score and current debt levels.

Planning Tip:
Start a "Hidden Costs" fund. Experts suggest saving 1% of the home's value every year for repairs and maintenance.
