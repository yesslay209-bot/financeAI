What is a Budget?

A budget is a plan for your money. It tells your money where to go instead of wondering where it went.

Simple Budget Categories

  • Income: Money you earn from work or side gigs.
  • Needs: Things you must pay (rent, utilities, groceries).
  • Wants: Things you enjoy (movies, dining out, hobbies).
  • Savings: Money for your future self or emergencies.

Easy Starter Tip

Save even $10 a week. Small habits grow into big money. Consistency is more important than the amount when you're just starting.
