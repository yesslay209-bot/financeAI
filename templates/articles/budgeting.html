What is a Budget?

A budget is a plan for your money. It tells your money where to go instead of wondering where it went.

Simple Budget Categories

  • Income (money you earn)
  • Needs (rent, food, bills)
  • Wants (fun, games, eating out)
  • Savings (money you keep)

Easy Starter Tip

Save even $10 a week. Small habits grow into big money.
